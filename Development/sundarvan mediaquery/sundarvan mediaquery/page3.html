    Puppetry Show






    Puppetry in India is said to have originated in early 5th century BC. Puppetry is the most innovative and entertaining way of expressing and telling a story. It is one of the best ways to stimulate the imagination and emotional development of an individual. But puppetry seems to be on the decline with the rapid spread of the digital media

    Here at Sundarvan, we try our best to preserve this traditional art of puppetry.

    Charges: Rs. 2000 (30-35 minutes)
